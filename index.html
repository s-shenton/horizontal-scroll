Fine-tuning

Fine-tuning makes an LLM more useful in specific domains by training them on curated content, like our policies or product FAQs, so they deliver more relevant results.

RAG

While LLMs rely on what they learned during training, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) lets the model pull in real-time info from external sources —like a knowledge base — as it responds. It's kind of like giving the AI access to a curated library right before it answers your question.

Agentic AI

You may have heard of Agentic AI — also called AI Agents. These are specialized versions of GenAI tools designed to act more autonomously. You can assign them a role, give them goals, and let them follow step-by-step logic to help you complete a task.
